Two players game

How to play the game

  • Each turn, a player roll a die as many time as he/she wishes. Each result added to his/her round score (current).
  • The active player has background color →
  • The winning player background color changes to →
  • if a player roll is 1, his/her current score get lost. After that, it is the next player's turn.
  • A wise player press "hold" when he/she has high current score, which will check if the total score of any of the players has reached 100. If none, the present player current score will be added to his/her global score. After that, it is the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 points on the global score is the winner.
  • Lastly, You can cancel the present game by clicking new game button but both players will loose their scores.
