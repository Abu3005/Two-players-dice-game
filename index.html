Two players game

How to play the game

  • Each turn, a player roll a die as many time as he/she wishes. Each result added to his/her round score (current).
  • But if the player roll is 1, all his/her ROUND score get lost. After that, it is the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to "hold", which means that his/her ROUND score get added to his/her global score. After that, it is the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 50 points on the global score is the winner.
  • Lastly, You can cancel the present game by clicking new game button.
